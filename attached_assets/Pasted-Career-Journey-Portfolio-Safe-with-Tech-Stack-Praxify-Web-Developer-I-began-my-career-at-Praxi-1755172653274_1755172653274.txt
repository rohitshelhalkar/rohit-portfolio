Tech Stack: Java, JavaScript, Typescript, React, Node.js, NestJS, PostgreSQL, Hasura (GraphQL), Stripe, Mailgun, Twilio, Gmail/Outlook APIs, WebSockets, Power BI, BigQuery, Cube.js, JWT
